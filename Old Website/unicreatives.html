Uni Creative Co.

Role: Design Lead

Tools: Figma

Duration: October 2019 to Present

Working with a group of passionate self-starters to create a platform and community for all creators (designers, photographers, musicians, etc.) at Cornell. As a design lead, I develop the brand identity of Uni and create assets for web and marketing that are consistent with the branding guidelines.

What Uni Does

  1. Help student creators gain experience by connecting them with on-campus gigs and organizations
  2. Provide a platform to promote and recognize the work of student creators
  3. Organize events to help creators meet each other and find people to collab with
